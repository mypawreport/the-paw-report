Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

1. Data We Collect

Account data: Email address, name, and password (encrypted) provided at registration.

Pet profile data: Pet name, breed, and size — stored to personalise ground temperature thresholds and safety alerts.

Location data: GPS coordinates collected in real time when you use the App. We do not store your location history beyond your current session unless you have walk history enabled (Gold tier).

Usage data: App interactions, feature usage, and session duration collected anonymously to improve the App.

Payment data: Billing is handled by our payment processor. We do not store full card numbers or sensitive payment details on our servers.

Referral data: Referral codes and associated account links used to apply rewards.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide real-time ground temperature, area safety, and walk recommendations
  • To personalise alerts based on your pet's size and profile
  • To process subscription payments and manage your account
  • To send push notifications about dangerous walking conditions (if opted in)
  • To improve App performance and fix bugs
  • To administer the referral programme

3. Location Data

Location access is required for core App functionality. We request location only while the App is in use. We do not sell, share, or transmit your precise location to third parties except as required to fetch weather and safety data (coordinates are sent to our backend API only). Gold tier walk history stores location summaries — you can delete this at any time from your profile.

4. Push Notifications

If you opt in to push notifications, we may send alerts when ground temperatures become dangerous, extreme weather is detected, or area safety changes significantly. You can opt out at any time in your device settings or app profile.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Payment processors — to handle billing securely

  • Analytics tools — anonymised usage data only
  • Law enforcement — only if required by law

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at mypawreport@gmail.com. Walk history (Gold tier) is retained for 30 days then automatically purged.

7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/HTTPS) for all data in transit. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We conduct regular security reviews of our infrastructure.
